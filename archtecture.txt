PROJECT GOAL
==================================================

Build an AI-first Commerce Platform consisting of three intelligent modules:

1. Shopping AI
2. Inventory AI
3. Customer Support AI

The system should be modular, scalable, event-driven, and production-ready.

This is NOT a chatbot application.

Each AI module should act as an intelligent business service capable of making recommendations, predictions, and automating workflows.
